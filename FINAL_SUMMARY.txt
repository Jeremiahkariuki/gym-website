📋 DATABASE MODELS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Member
├── full_name (TextField)
├── phone (CharField - unique)
├── email (EmailField)
├── address (CharField)
├── joined_on (DateField - auto)
└── membership_Plan (ForeignKey)

MembershipPlan
├── name (CharField)
├── price (DecimalField)
└── duration_days (PositiveIntegerField)

Membership
├── member (ForeignKey)
├── plan (ForeignKey)
├── start_date (DateField)
├── end_date (DateField - auto-calculated)
└── is_active (BooleanField)

Payment
├── member (ForeignKey)
├── Membership (ForeignKey)
├── amount (DecimalField)
├── paid_on (DateTimeField)
├── method (CharField)
├── reference (CharField)
└── date (DateField)

Attendance
├── member (ForeignKey)
└── date (DateTimeField)
